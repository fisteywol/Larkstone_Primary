Welcome to Larkstone Primary

Larkstone Primary is a welcoming Roblox school where students can learn, make friends, attend lessons and progress through their educational journey.

Join Today

Larkstone Primary School is an excellent, welcoming, and nurturing primary school that aims to offer all children a safe and enriching learning environment. It seeks to ignite curiosity, foster creativity, and build confidence in a comprehensive and balanced curriculum that ensures that children realize their full potential. The staff at our school works in partnership with our pupils' families and the larger community to ensure that our pupils achieve high standards academically, behave positively, and develop as individuals. At Larkstone Primary School, respect, kindness, perseverance, and responsibility are key values.
